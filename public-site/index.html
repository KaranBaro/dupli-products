Frequently asked questions

Does Dupli Products store my product data?

No. Product data is read from and written to your Shopify store through Shopify Admin API. The app only stores session data required for authentication.

Can I duplicate products in bulk?

Yes. You can duplicate selected products or use tag, product type, and collection as bulk sources.

Can I copy images and metafields?

Yes. The app includes options to copy images and metafields when duplicating products.

How do I contact support?

You can contact us by email or WhatsApp from the support page.
