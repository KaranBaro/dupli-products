Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Dupli Products ("we", "our", "us") is a Shopify application that helps merchants duplicate products quickly and efficiently. This Privacy Policy explains how we access, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Access

When you install and use Dupli Products, we may access the following data via Shopify's Admin API:

  • Product Data: Titles, descriptions, variants, prices, images, tags, metafields, and product status — used to duplicate products.
  • Store Information: Store name and domain for authentication and app functionality.
  • Session Data: Shopify OAuth tokens to securely maintain your session.
  • Collections & Product Types: Used for bulk duplication features.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Customer personal data (names, emails, addresses)
  • Payment or billing information
  • Storefront browsing or tracking data
  • Any personal customer behavior data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to provide the app’s functionality:

  • Duplicating products (single and bulk)
  • Copying images, metafields, tags, and variants
  • Filtering products by tag, type, or collection
  • Authenticating your Shopify session

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

We store only essential session data (OAuth tokens) securely using industry-standard encryption. Product data is not stored permanently on our servers — all operations happen directly via Shopify's API.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • All session data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.
  • We comply with Shopify's required data privacy webhooks:
    • customers/data_request
    • customers/redact
    • shop/redact

6. Data Sharing

Your data is never shared with third parties. Data flows only between:

  • Your Shopify store
  • Our application server
  • Shopify APIs

7. Your Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction
  • Request deletion

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a revised date.
